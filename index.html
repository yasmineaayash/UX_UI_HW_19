Canada Council for the Arts
Redesign

The Canada Council for the Arts is a federal organization that supports, promotes, and celebrates the arts in Canada. It provides funding and grants to artists and arts organizations across the country, as well as supports public engagement with the arts through various programs and initiatives. The Canada Council for the Arts aims to foster a vibrant and diverse arts community in Canada that reflects the country's cultural richness and diversity.

The Stop
Redesign

The Stop is a community-driven organization based in Toronto, Canada, committed to addressing food insecurity through innovative programs such as community kitchens and healthy food markets. They believe that access to nutritious food is a basic human right and are dedicated to creating a healthier and more resilient community.

Fridge Friend

Developed an app that tackles food waste and tracks the shelf life of food in our fridge, alerting us when our ingredients are about to go bad and suggesting recipes to utilize our leftovers while keeping our fridge healthy.

Marshal Me

"Marshal Me" is a travel app that assists you with your organizational process of packing only what is necessary, prepping & storing your travel documents prior to your flight and having everything you need for stress-free trips. The app will take into consideration the weather at the destination, your planned activities and other preferences to generate a packing list best suited for your trip.
